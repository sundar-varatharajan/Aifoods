About Ai Foods

From wedding halls to wholesale counters.

Ai Foods supplies fresh, hot South Indian food in bulk quantities. Every batch is cooked to order and packed to stay hot until it reaches you. Whether it's a wedding reception, a corporate event or a daily wholesale order, we prepare everything fresh and deliver it on schedule.

Based in Thiruvallur, we serve Chennai and nearby areas. We work directly with event organizers, caterers and businesses that need reliable, large quantity South Indian food without compromising on taste or freshness.
